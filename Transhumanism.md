# Definitions
> trans·hu·man·ism  
> [tranzˈ(h)yo͞oməˌnizəm, tran(t)sˈ(h)yo͞oməˌnizəm]  
> noun  
> transhumanism (noun)  
> the belief or theory that the human race can evolve beyond its current physical and mental limitations, especially by means of science and technology.
